For the best accuracy (and to prevent from memorization), have someone to assist you when testing your vision with the eye chart. If you use eyeglasses or contact lenses for driving or other distance vision tasks, wear them during the test.

  1. Place the chart on a wall or easel 10 feet away.
  2. Cover one eye with your hands, a large spoon or some other item that completely blocks the vision of the covered eye.
  3. Identify a line on the chart you can comfortably read. Read the letters on that line aloud. Have your assistant stand near the chart and record your accuracy
  4. Switch to the other eye and repeat.

This is a pretend eye examination.

Consult your doctor for a legitimate one.
